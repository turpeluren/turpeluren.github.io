The story of a drawing I made trying to fuck with law-systems.

With signatures

My idea

I wrote down in my notes to make an art piece like a contract. With a signature you would be proclaimed the "rightful" owner.

The path

Firstly, I signed it myself to claim initial ownership. Then hung it on the wall of our (my two friends and I) apartment in Barcelona. When we had friends over I hoped they would find it interesting but it took a while until Bianca signed during a party. I later heard from her that their french neighbor had signed and maybe? brought it to France.
